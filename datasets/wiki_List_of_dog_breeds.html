List of extant and extinct dogs
Selection of different dog breeds

This list of dog breeds includes both extant and extinct breeds, varieties and dog types. A research article on dog genomics published in Science/AAAS defines modern dog breeds as "a recent invention defined by conformation to a physical ideal and purity of lineage."[1]
